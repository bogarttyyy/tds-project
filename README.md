Prototype for a 2D survival game with enemies, shooting mechanic, inventory system, and going inside buildings concept.

## Controls:

(WASD + Shift) - Walk / Run

(E) - Pickup Item

(LeftClick) - Shoot

(Tab) - Inventory

(R) - Reset Scene


## Instructions:
Control your character and move around to pickup items.

Coins can be picked up by just walking over it, while other items needs to be manually picked up by pressing E

Once you have a gun, you can left click to shoot

Enemies are colored pick but don't really do anything beside stand and be shot

Walk through the door to enter room

If a door is locked, your character will try to unlock the door by trying to go through it, a progress bar will appear until the door is unlock

You can press to open/close your inventory (at the moment it does nothing but see what's inside)
